
## Mass Children's Examination 

A lightweight QOL mod to send all your lazy children to take the Children's exam in Celestial or Meritocratic Governments. 
Adds a decision to send all eligible children to take the exams when there are eligible children

fixes:
- proper cost subtracting
- cooldown on exam failure
